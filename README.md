## About Me

I'm a graduate student and software engineer with a background that spans enterprise Salesforce engineering and modern full-stack development. I completed my BE in Computer Science at Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology, and I'm currently finishing my MS in Computer Science at the University of Illinois Chicago (December 2025).

Before grad school, I spent over a year at **Deloitte** as an Analyst, building production Salesforce Health Cloud solutions for enterprise healthcare clients — Apex, Lightning Web Components, REST/SOAP integrations, and release pipelines that shipped real, regulated systems.

Right now I'm focused on full-stack and backend engineering, cloud infrastructure, and applied AI — particularly agentic systems, RAG pipelines, and LLM-powered applications. I like building things that are technically sound end to end: clean architecture, solid data models, and interfaces people actually enjoy using.
